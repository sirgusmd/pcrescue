PC Rescue A friendly guide for life after Windows 10

Give your computer a second life

Windows 10 stopped getting security updates in October 2025 — but if your computer still works well, there's no need to throw it away or buy a new one.

This free tool helps you find a safe, fast, and free replacement system that keeps you browsing, emailing, and watching videos just like before. We'll ask a few simple questions about your computer and what you use it for, then suggest the best fit — explained in plain English.
